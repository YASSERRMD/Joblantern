# Research Partnership Program

Universities studying migration, labor economics, and fraud should
be able to partner with Joblantern under structured agreements that
protect privacy and advance scholarship.

## Tracks

- **Aggregated** — public anonymised dashboards, no agreement needed.
- **Pseudonymised** — agreement-bound, no direct identifiers.
- **Restricted** — sandboxed replica access, ethics review required.

## Process

1. Submit a research proposal to the annual [CFP](RESEARCH-CFP.md).
2. Research advisory board reviews and rates proposals.
3. Selected proposals sign the appropriate [data-sharing agreement](DSA-TEMPLATES.md).
4. The IRB / ethics checklist is completed.
5. The sandbox is provisioned.
6. Publication credits Joblantern per the attribution norms.

## Annual cadence

- CFP opens: 1 March.
- Decisions: 1 May.
- Sandboxes provisioned: 1 June.
