## Example case studies (illustrative)

### Wage-Deception in the Gulf Corridor

Partner: A European labour-economics group
Period: 2026-2027
Tier: C
Question: Does pre-departure intervention reduce wage deception?
Method: Difference-in-differences across two corridors.
Finding: Statistically significant reduction in promised-vs-received wage gap.
What we changed: Tightened the salary-band rule pack and added
country-specific wage benchmarks.
Output: Joint preprint published under [attribution norms](RESEARCH-ATTRIBUTION.md).
