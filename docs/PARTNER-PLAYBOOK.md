# Partner Playbook

This playbook is for the NGO operator who has just become a Joblantern partner.

## Week 1

- Deploy your instance.
- Run your first verdict.
- Localise the verdict page for your primary user language.
- Attend the next office-hours session.

## Week 2

- Open a referral channel with the worker-support NGO closest to you.
- Decide your verdict-publication policy (private dashboard vs. NGO-wide intel sharing).
- Configure intake forms for your top user-acquisition channel (WhatsApp / kiosk / SMS).

## Month 1

- Submit your first monthly metrics ([metrics](../internal/capacity/metrics/partner.go)).
- Attend the regional working-group meeting.
- Identify two volunteers to learn the operator role.

## Quarter 1

- Run your first appeals.
- Consider applying to the [research-partnership programme](RESEARCH-PROGRAM.md).
- Apply to the small-grants programme if applicable.

## Reach out

- Office hours per [schedule](../internal/capacity/officehours/schedule.go).
- Mentor (assigned at signup).
- Regional coordinator.
