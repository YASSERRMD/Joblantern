# Bilateral Agreement Template — Joblantern × Regulator

> **Status**: template. Adapt jurisdiction-specific clauses before
> execution. Always run through the regulator's legal counsel and
> Joblantern's [Trust & Safety Council](GOVERNANCE.md).

## 1. Parties

- **Joblantern**: the operating entity hosting the instance.
- **Regulator**: the agency, ministry, or consumer-protection body.

## 2. Purpose

The Parties agree to collaborate on combating recruitment fraud,
labor trafficking, and migrant exploitation in a manner that:

- protects the privacy of individual applicants,
- respects the sovereignty of the Regulator's jurisdiction, and
- preserves Joblantern's editorial independence.

## 3. Data flows

| Flow                                  | Direction          | Lawful basis (template) |
|---------------------------------------|--------------------|--------------------------|
| Official blacklist                    | Regulator → JL     | Public interest |
| Official whitelist (licensees)        | Regulator → JL     | Public interest |
| Aggregated national stats             | JL → Regulator     | Performance of task |
| Consented complaint forwarding        | JL → Regulator     | Explicit consent |
| Annual transparency report            | JL → Public        | Legitimate interest |

## 4. Independence

Joblantern retains sole editorial control over verdict logic.
Regulator-supplied data is weighted, not blindly trusted.

## 5. Confidentiality

Mutual NDA applies to non-public threat intelligence shared under
this MOU. Aggregated and anonymized data may be published.

## 6. Term and termination

Initial term: 24 months. Either party may terminate with 90 days
notice. Data shared before termination remains usable under the
purpose limitation above.

## 7. Audit & transparency

Annual joint review of metrics, false-positive rates, and any
appeals received against blacklist entries.

## 8. Governing law & dispute resolution

To be filled in per jurisdiction. Default: seat of arbitration in a
neutral jurisdiction mutually agreed.
