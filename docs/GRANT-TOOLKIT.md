# Grant-Application Toolkit for Partner NGOs

Reusable language and evidence so partner NGOs can fund their
Joblantern-based programmes without reinventing the brief.

## Re-usable problem statement

> "Recruitment fraud and predatory labor recruitment cost migrant
> workers an estimated USD ___ each year. Our programme uses
> Joblantern, an open, NGO-led system, to verify employment offers
> before vulnerable workers commit to travel."

## Logic model

| Inputs       | Activities                  | Outputs                                | Outcomes                              |
|--------------|-----------------------------|----------------------------------------|---------------------------------------|
| Staff hours  | Operate the instance        | Verdicts per month                     | Workers warned before paying agents   |
| Partner refs | Outreach in target corridors| Workshops attended                     | Behaviour change in destination focus |
| Funding      | Localise content            | Languages supported                    | Reach in under-served languages       |

## Metrics partners report

- Verdicts delivered.
- Confirmed scams stopped (worker self-reported).
- Average time from submission to verdict.
- Cost-per-verdict from [costtrack](../internal/scale/costtrack/cost.go).

## Boilerplate "why open" paragraph

> Joblantern is open-source and governed by an independent trust &
> safety council with NGO, technical, and academic seats. Our
> programme contributes back: every confirmed scam strengthens the
> shared model that every partner uses.

## Attachment pack

- Annual transparency report.
- Bias audit summary.
- Compliance pack (GDPR / DPDPA / PDPL).
