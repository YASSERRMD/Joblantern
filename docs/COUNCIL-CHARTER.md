# Trust & Safety Council — Charter

## Mission

The Council exists to make accountable the hard decisions Joblantern
otherwise risks making in private: who is a scammer, what counts as
evidence, who runs federated instances, and when an active-defense
action is justified.

## Scope

The Council:

1. **Oversees** the appeals process for any entity Joblantern labels.
2. **Reviews** active-defense packets before they leave the building.
3. **Sets policy** on dual-use data sharing with regulators.
4. **Publishes** an annual transparency report.

## Composition

- Three NGO seats (one each from Asia, Africa, Latin America).
- Two technical seats (cryptography, ML).
- Two academic seats (migration policy, law).
- One regulator seat (rotating, observer-only voting).
- One Joblantern engineering seat.

## Quorum & decisions

- Standing decisions: simple majority of seated members.
- Emergency takedowns: 3-of-5 fast-track, ratified within 72 hours.
- Charter amendments: 2/3 majority + public comment period.

## Independence

The Council is independent of any single funder. See [FUNDING](FUNDING.md).

## Term

24-month terms, staggered so half the council rotates each year.
