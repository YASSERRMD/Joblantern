# Pre-Registration Support

We encourage pre-registration so research findings stay credible and
falsifiable.

## What to register

- Research question & primary hypothesis.
- Outcome variable(s) and how they are computed from Joblantern data.
- Inclusion criteria for verdicts (date range, country, industry).
- Analysis plan and statistical tests.
- Stopping rule, if any.

## Where to register

Any of OSF (https://osf.io), AsPredicted (https://aspredicted.org),
or your institution's repository. Joblantern stores a copy of the
PDF hash in the partnership record.

## Deviations from plan

Documented but not penalised. Pre-registration is about transparency,
not constraint.
