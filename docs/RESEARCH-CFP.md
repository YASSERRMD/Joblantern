# Open Call for Research Proposals

> Annual. CFP opens 1 March, closes 1 April, decisions 1 May.

## Topics we want to see studied

- Recruitment-fraud prevalence by corridor.
- Effectiveness of pre-departure information.
- Wage-deception measurement.
- Network analysis of recruitment intermediaries.
- Bias and fairness in algorithmic verdicts.
- Long-term labour outcomes of workers who used Joblantern vs. who did not.

## What we provide

- Sandboxed Postgres replica (Tier C) or pseudonymised snapshots (Tier B).
- Joblantern engineering office hours.
- Co-promotion of published results.

## What we ask

- Pre-registration where appropriate.
- Open-access publication where possible.
- Attribution per [norms](RESEARCH-ATTRIBUTION.md).
- A short "what would you tell other researchers" reflection at end of project.

## Application

Submit via [`research-cfp@joblantern.org`](mailto:research-cfp@joblantern.org)
with a 2-page proposal.
