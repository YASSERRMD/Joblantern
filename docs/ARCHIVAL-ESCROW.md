# Archival Legal-Escrow Arrangement

Joblantern places the annual-archive custody under a legal-escrow
arrangement so the archives outlive the operating entity.

## Parties

- **Grantor**: Joblantern Foundation.
- **Escrow agent**: a neutral, jurisdictionally diverse trust.
- **Beneficiary**: the public, with a named successor entity for operational custody.

## Triggers for custody transfer

- Dissolution of the Joblantern Foundation.
- A material breach of the [council charter](COUNCIL-CHARTER.md).
- A finding by an independent auditor that the Foundation can no
  longer maintain the archive.

## Materials held in escrow

- Copies of every annual archive.
- The DataCite DOI prefix authorisation.
- Cryptographic signing keys used for archive integrity.
- Contact lists for the published mirror destinations.

## Successor entity

Named in a sealed annex updated annually. The annex names a list of
fallbacks; the agent opens them in order until one accepts.

## Independence from funders

The escrow agent is independent of any current or recent Joblantern
funder. See [FUNDING](FUNDING.md).
