# Publication Credit & Attribution Norms

## Cite

When citing Joblantern data:

```
Joblantern Verdict Archive, <year>, doi:10.<archive-doi>.
```

The DOI is assigned via DataCite under the [archival policy](ARCHIVAL.md).

## Acknowledge

Include in the acknowledgments section:

> The authors thank the Joblantern Foundation and the partner NGOs
> whose verdicts made this work possible.

## Co-authorship

Joblantern engineers are not co-authors by default. Co-authorship is
appropriate when they contributed substantively to study design or
analysis, per ICMJE.

## Pre-publication review

For Tier C, Joblantern reviews drafts only for re-identification risk
before publication. Findings, framing, and tone are out of scope.

## Embargoes

Joblantern does not embargo findings. Researchers may pre-announce on
their schedule; Joblantern will re-share when asked.
